Crackin Collard Greens

by Jade Brown

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches of collard greens
  • 1 pack of smoked turkey necks
  • 1 whole diced onion (your choice of onion)
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1 carton of unsalted chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of pepperocini juice
  • 1 whole pepperocini's

Directions

  1. Chop bunches of collard greens
  2. bunches of greens to clean sink or big bowl
  3. Add cold water and baking soda to the sink or bowl
  4. Thoroughly clean collard greens until water is clear
  5. Drain collard greens and set aside until it is time to add to pot
  6. Set stove top to medium
  7. Add oil to preferred pot
  8. Add diced onion and minced garlic
  9. Lightly brown diced onion and minced garlic
  10. Add unsalted chicken broth
  11. Add smoked turkey necks
  12. Let simmer on low heat for an 1hr and 30 mins (or until tender)
  13. Remove smoked neck bones and separate meat from the bones
  14. Add smoked neck meat back into the pot
  15. Add chopped and cleaned collard greens into pot
  16. Add the collard greens in small amounts so that they can properly cook down
  17. Once all greens have been added place lid over pot
  18. Let collard greens cook for 1 - 2 hours or until preferred softness
