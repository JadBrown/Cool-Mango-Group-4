Spaghetti

by Jade Brown

Ingredients

  • 1 box of angel hair pasta
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 large jar of garlic roasted pasta sauce
  • half a cup of shredded parmesean cheese
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • choice of cooking oil

Directions

  • Put pot of water on high
  • Put angel hair pasta in water when water begins to boil
  • Cook pasta until desired softness
  • Drain pasta and let sit
  • Cook ground beef on medium
  • Add pasta sauce
  • Add pasta
  • Mix and let cook for additional 10 minutes
  • Add desired amount of parmesean cheese
  • Plate and serve!
